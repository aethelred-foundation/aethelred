The Problem

High performance, low trust.
AI systems need cryptographic accountability.

Today, most high-value inference happens inside opaque cloud boundaries. Enterprises cannot prove which model ran, how data was handled, or whether output was tampered with before delivery.

Difficult to Audit

Centralized AI APIs are impossible to audit at the protocol layer. No cryptographic proof binds a specific model to a specific output.

Difficult to Govern

Enterprises in regulated markets — healthcare, finance, defense — cannot satisfy compliance requirements when inference is a black box.

Difficult to Localise

Strict jurisdictions (UAE, EU, KSA) require verifiable computation that respects data sovereignty. Current AI infrastructure cannot enforce this.
