Token Utility

AETHEL serves multiple critical functions within the Aethelred ecosystem, creating natural demand and value accrual.

01

Inference Payments

All AI computation requests on the Aethelred network are paid for in AETHEL tokens. Users purchase inference credits, which are burned upon use, creating deflationary pressure proportional to network usage. Enterprise corridors offer bulk pricing with locked token commitments.

02

Validator Staking

Validators must stake a minimum of 100,000 AETHEL to participate in the Proof-of-Useful-Work consensus. Staked tokens are locked for a minimum of 90 days and serve as collateral against malicious behavior. Higher stakes increase validator selection probability.

03

Governance Rights

AETHEL holders can vote on protocol upgrades, fee structures, slashing parameters, and ecosystem fund allocations. Voting power is proportional to staked tokens with time-weighted bonuses for long-term stakers. One token equals one vote in all governance decisions.

04

Seal Verification Fees

Third parties verifying Digital Seals pay micro-fees in AETHEL. While individual fees are minimal (0.001 AETHEL per verification), high-volume verification use cases generate significant protocol revenue. 50% of verification fees are burned.

05

Model Registry Access

AI model providers pay AETHEL to register their models on the Aethelred Model Registry. Registration fees are tiered based on model size and expected usage. Registered models receive unique identifiers used in Digital Seals for provenance tracking.

06

Priority Access

During high-demand periods, users can pay premium AETHEL fees for priority inference processing. Priority fees are distributed to validators processing the request. Enterprise corridor subscribers receive guaranteed priority without additional fees.

Value Accrual Mechanisms

Multiple mechanisms ensure AETHEL captures value as the Aethelred network grows.

Fee Burning

10% of all inference fees are permanently burned, reducing total supply over time. At current usage rates, approximately 2.5M AETHEL are burned monthly, creating sustainable deflationary pressure.

Staking Lock-up

84.7% of circulating supply is currently staked with minimum 90-day lock periods. This reduces liquid supply and creates natural price stability during market volatility.

Usage-Driven Demand

Every AI inference requires AETHEL payment. As network usage grows, natural demand increases. Enterprise corridor commitments lock tokens for 1-3 year periods, further reducing circulating supply.

Validator Requirements

Operating a validator requires 100,000 AETHEL minimum stake. As network demand grows, more validators are needed, increasing total tokens locked in the validator set.

Governance Power

Meaningful governance participation requires substantial token holdings. As the protocol grows in importance, demand for governance influence drives token accumulation by serious stakeholders.

Model Registry Fees

AI model providers pay ongoing AETHEL fees for registry listing. Premium placements and featured model status require additional token commitments, creating B2B demand independent of end-user inference.
