How to Participate

Any AETHEL token holder can participate in governance. Your voting power is proportional to your token holdings, with bonuses for long-term staking.

  • Hold AETHEL tokens in a supported wallet
  • Connect to the Governance Portal
  • Vote directly or delegate to a trusted representative
  • Earn governance rewards for participation

Voting Power Calculation

Voting power includes base token holdings plus time-weighted bonuses for staked tokens, incentivizing long-term alignment.

  • Base: 1 AETHEL = 1 vote
  • 90-day stake: 1.25x multiplier
  • 180-day stake: 1.5x multiplier
  • 365-day stake: 2x multiplier
