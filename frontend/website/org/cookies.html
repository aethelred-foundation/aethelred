Cookie policy and preference controls.

Effective date: March 5, 2026. This page explains the cookie categories available on Aethelred, the essential cookies currently in use, and how future optional settings connect to the privacy policy and terms of use.

Categories

How Aethelred uses cookies

Essential cookies support the live production site today. Analytics and marketing categories are reserved for future optional tooling and remain inactive unless Aethelred deploys those services and you explicitly opt in.

Essential cookies

Required for navigation, security protections, and baseline functionality. These cannot be disabled.

Analytics cookies

Reserved for future aggregate measurement tooling. These remain off unless such services are introduced and you opt in.

Marketing cookies

Reserved for future outreach and attribution workflows. These remain off unless those services are introduced and you opt in.

Retention

How preferences are stored

Cookie choices stay in browser storage until changed or cleared, including preferences for optional categories that may be activated in future site releases.

Preference retention

Cookie choices are stored locally in your browser until changed or cleared. Even when optional categories are inactive, your preferences remain available for future Aethelred site services.
