Stablecoin Diligence

What institutions should understand about the settlement layer

Aethelred's stablecoin surface is framed as institutional routing infrastructure, not as a launched retail product. The current diligence focus is the control model, reserve evidence path, and relayer plus governance economics.

What is source-backed today

Zero-liquidity-pool design

The whitepaper and tokenomics explicitly position routing as issuer-authorized rather than pool-based.

Two current routing modes

CCTP V2 covers Circle burn-and-mint flows and TEE Issuer Mint covers non-CCTP assets.

500,000 AETHEL relayer bond

Message relay is economically bounded instead of operating as an unbonded service role.

3-of-5 institutional governance

Critical operations sit behind a five-key governance model with a minimum 7-day action delay in the contract path.

What is intentionally not claimed

No public issuer roster

The site does not publish live issuer, custodian, or settlement-partner names.

No public mainnet asset registry

There is no public production asset table with final addresses and domains.

No retail wallet framing

The published surface is for institutional routing and control, not consumer wallet distribution.

No fee-sheet marketing claim

This is a governance and risk-control diligence topic today, not a published commercial pricing page.
