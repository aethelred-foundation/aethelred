Legal

Privacy policy for Aethelred digital properties.

Effective date: March 5, 2026. This policy explains what we collect, why we process it, and the controls available to users, together with the terms of use and cookie disclosures.

Overview

Policy scope and accountability

Who this applies to

This policy applies to visitors, community participants, and institutional contacts who interact with Aethelred websites and communication channels.

Controller details

Aethelred Foundation acts as data controller for website operations. Contact: privacy@aethelred.org.

Collection

Information we collect

Collection is limited to operational, contact, and consent data needed to run Aethelred websites, handle requests, and honor the cookie choices made in your browser.

Data we collect

  • Contact and institution details submitted through forms.
  • Technical metadata such as IP-derived security hashes, browser type, and referring URL.
  • Cookie preferences and related consent signals.
Usage

How collected information is used

Use is restricted to site operations, communications, abuse prevention, and diligence workflows described in this policy and the terms of use.

How data is used

  • To respond to investor, partner, and governance inquiries.
  • To maintain security, abuse detection, and service reliability.
  • To improve website performance and product communication workflows.
Rights

Rights and contact channels

You can request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of personal data where applicable by law, and you can separately adjust browser-level cookie preferences.

Your rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of personal data where applicable by law. Email requests to privacy@aethelred.org.
