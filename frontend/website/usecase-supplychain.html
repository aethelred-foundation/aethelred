Industry Challenges

Supply Chain Opacity Crisis

Complex global supply chains lack visibility, enabling counterfeits, compliance violations, and ESG failures.

Counterfeit Infiltration

$2.2 trillion in counterfeit goods enter supply chains annually. Traditional track-and-trace systems are easily spoofed without cryptographic verification.

Origin Verification

Regulations like UFLPA require proof of origin, but paper-based certificates are easily forged. True geographic provenance requires spatial attestation.

ESG Compliance

Scope 3 emissions reporting and sustainability claims require verifiable data from suppliers, but self-reported data lacks credibility.
