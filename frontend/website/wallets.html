What is an Aethel Wallet?

An Aethel wallet is your gateway to the Aethelred network. It's a digital tool that lets you store AETHEL tokens, sign transactions, manage sealed computation credentials, and interact with smart contracts.

Unlike traditional crypto wallets, Aethel wallets also handle verification credentials — cryptographic certificates that prove you have access to specific sealed AI computations.

  • Hold and transfer AETHEL tokens
  • Store verification credentials
  • Connect to dApps and smart contracts
  • Participate in governance voting

Stay Safe

Wallet Security Tips

Protect your assets with these essential security practices.

🔑

Backup Your Seed

Write down your recovery phrase and store it securely offline. Never share it with anyone.

🔒

Use Hardware Wallets

For large holdings, use a hardware wallet to keep private keys completely offline.

🔍

Verify Transactions

Always double-check addresses and amounts before signing any transaction.

🚫

Beware of Scams

Aethelred will never ask for your seed phrase. Report suspicious messages.
