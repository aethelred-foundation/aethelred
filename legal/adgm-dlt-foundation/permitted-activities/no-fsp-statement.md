## Statement

[Foundation Legal Name] is established as a DLT Foundation under the Abu Dhabi Global Market (ADGM) Foundations Regulations 2017. The Foundation does not hold a Financial Services Permission (FSP) from the ADGM Financial Services Regulatory Authority (FSRA).

The Foundation's activities are limited to those permitted for DLT Foundations under ADGM guidance, including but not limited to:

- Development and maintenance of open-source distributed ledger technology
- Governance of protocol parameters and upgrade processes
- Distribution of grants for ecosystem development
- [Additional permitted activities as confirmed by legal counsel]

The Foundation does not:

- Provide financial services as defined under ADGM Financial Services and Markets Regulations
- Operate as an exchange, broker, custodian, or money service business
- Provide investment advice or portfolio management services
- Issue securities or financial instruments
- [Additional exclusions as confirmed by legal counsel]

Where ecosystem applications built on the protocol involve regulated activities (such as payments, custody, or trading), those activities are performed by independently licensed entities and not by the Foundation.
