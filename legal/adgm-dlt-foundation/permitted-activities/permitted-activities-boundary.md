## Classification Key

- **Permitted without FSP:** Activity falls within DLT Foundation permitted activities
- **Requires licensed counterparty:** Foundation may facilitate, but regulated activity must be performed by licensed entity
- **Requires authorization:** Foundation itself would need FSP or other authorization
- **Prohibited as currently structured:** Activity cannot proceed without structural changes
- **[Requires legal analysis]:** Classification not yet determined — legal counsel must review
